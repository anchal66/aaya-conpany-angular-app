METHOD TO MADNESS

  • We collect post-consumer non-biodegradable waste either in mixed form or segregated based on type (glass, metal, paper, plastic and fabric) from designated pick up points.

  • At the time of collection, the waste is weighed, records ore mode (by us and our client).

  • The collected material then enters our network of formalised Collection Partners

  • Bulk Waste Generators that generate high volumes of waste are rewarded for practicing proper segregation - through Cashbacks or stationery mode from waste.

We invite residential, commercial, educational, industrial as well as religious establishments to reach out for a systematic assessment of your waste generation.
